Principal's Message



Eduford College was established with a humble vision of imparting education to local youths. In the course of fulfilling that vision, the college made remarkable achievements and emerged as one of the premier institutions in the country. We have been accredited with "A" grade by the NAAC. We owe these achievements to the guidance of the enlightened management, hard work of committed faculty and administrative staff and the active participation of students.
