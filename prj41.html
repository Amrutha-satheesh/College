Eduford University is one of the world’s leading academic and research institutions, offering a unique educational experience that prepares the next generation of global citizens to lead and make a difference in the world. We are a vibrant community of exceptional students, faculty, alumni and professionals dedicated to real-world applications of our research, scholarship, faith and service. Established in 1973, Eduford is the nation’s oldest Catholic and Jesuit university. Drawing upon the 450-year-old legacy of Jesuit education, we provide students with a world-class learning experience focused on educating the whole person through exposure to different faiths, cultures and beliefs.







Established in 1973, Eduford College quickly attained a prominent position among the limited number of undergraduate colleges in U.S at that time. In these 50 years, the number of degree colleges in U.S has shown astronomical growth but Seshadripuram College continues to maintain its reputation and position. From a meagre strength of thirty students at inception, the student strength has grown substantially to more than 3000 with a commensurate expansion of the faculty. The Institution is first among the affiliated College under U.S University to be granted PG courses and to offer the BBA course. We are currently affiliated to U.S City University after the University trifurcation process

The institution has been Accredited with Highest Grade A++ by NAAC and regularly features among the top Commerce colleges not only in U.S but across the nation and is ranked high across the survey’s conducted by leading national newspapers. Our students regularly secure University ranks for their excellent academic and beyond academic performances.

The institutional reputation is sustained for more than 5 decades through its persistent commitment for Quality Learning along with adapting to the changing societal needs without losing the touch with core values. The college attempts to create a stimulating teaching learning environment which equips students with the knowledge and skills required to face the challenges of modern era along with instilling a deep sense of social responsibility and self-discipline among students. The Institution also intends to have a good, cordial and mutually supportive relationship with all its stake holders along with developing knowledge rich and value based citizen for sustained national progress.
