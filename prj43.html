ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

Admission is strictly based on merit basis.

Any student who has passed PUC/10+2/Pre-University equivalent courses in Science, Commerce and Arts background is eligible.

The selected students' list will be announced on the college notice board and Institutional Website.

If the student doesn't take admission within the prescribed time, the admission will be cancelled without further notice and the seat will be allotted to the next student on the merit list.
