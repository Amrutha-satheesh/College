Single Window System





The college has evolved a unique single window system of payment. This ensures that students do not need to stand in long queues from time to time to pay their exam fees, tuition fees, etc. A one-time payment saves time and energy for both students and the administration.

One time payments towards different fees, including university semester examination fees.

Fees include services such as issuing of certificates for various purposes to both current students and alumni.

Fees includes Comprehensive course manual prepared by faculty for every subject.

Fee includes use of the Internet in Digital Library and Computer Labs.

Fee includes use of all facilities of the college.
