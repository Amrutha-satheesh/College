Center For Global Excellence





CGE Overview



The Centre for Global Excellence is an initiative which aims to help our students acquire a global edge to their education. CGE provides our students opportunities for interface with distinguished faculty from prestigious institutions of higher learning abroad. Ultimately we hope to mould our students into citizens of the world equipped with relevant knowledge regarding the latest developments in the international scenario.





CGE Offers

An opportunity to present research papers in International conferences.

An opportunity to conduct joint research projects with our global partners.

Acquire invaluable international exposure as well as a certificate by participating in a study tour for 10-14 days to any of our partner institutions in UK or USA.

Obtain a valuable Postgraduate degree from any of our partner institutions after finishing degree in any stream. Students may choose the duration of the course abroad to be one/ two years. They may also enjoy gainful employment abroad during this time period.

Summer Research Internships abroad: Get an international study experience abroad,opt for a two-months certificate course at UCO.

A semester abroad: Students of our college can avail education at UCO for a period of four months after completing graduation from any stream.

This includes the very valuable - 'Productivity & Employablility Skills' course conducted by faculty from the Institute of Productivity, UK as well as French & Tally courses among others. CGE also conducts orientation programmes in culture & etiquette to better equip students going abroad in adapting to a foreign environment.
