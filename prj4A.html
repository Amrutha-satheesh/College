Master of Commerce in International Business (M. IB. Program)


The Master of Commerce in International Business (M.Com.I.B) is a postgraduate degree that teaches and exposes students to international business and its environment. It is unavoidable to be prepared with global competencies and tackle the current comprehensive level difficulties due to the paradigm shift in cultural diversity. Students will be taught global proficiencies and skills in this course.

The focus of the course is on a thorough examination, analysis, comprehension, and management of global business. Accounting, financial planning, economic theory, micro and macroeconomics, money and banking systems, business and trade from a global viewpoint are among the topics covered. After successfully completing the course, candidates will have a broad understanding of the features and policies of finance from a global perspective. This course enhances skills such as management, accounting, decision making, business management, and entrepreneurship by providing a deep understanding of diverse economies in order to establish, formulate, and execute effective global company strategies.

The Master of Commerce in International Business program's overall goal is to provide students with professional education and training. We work hard to give students an opportunity to hone their conceptual, practical, research-oriented, and analytical business abilities in International Business. Furthermore, our students are exposed to the difficulties that face the business, industry, and/or academic sectors.





Program Structure

The Master of Commerce Finance & Accounts in International Business (M.Com.I.B.) is a two-year (four semesters) program consisting of the following subjects:

I Semester

1.1 India’s Foreign Trade Policy & Business Negotiation

1.2 International Trade & Environment

1.3 Multi-Cultural Management

1.4 Global Legal Environment

1.5 Global resources

1.6 Foreign Language(European –I) French or German

1.7 SOFT CORE Communication Skills



II Semester

2.3 Management Information Systems

2.4 Impex Procedures & Documentation

2.5 Research Methodology

2.6 Foreign Language(European –I) French or German
