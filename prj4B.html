Bachelor of Commerce





The Department of Commerce and Management consistently helps to places the college among the Top 40 best Commerce colleges in the country. With a student strength of 2300 and 37 faculty, it is one of the largest in Bangalore University as well as in the newly constituted Bengaluru City University. There is a great demand for admissions, leading to stiff cut-off percentage and drawing students of the highest quality. The aim of the department is to ensure that every student gets an equal opportunity to become an enterprising and productive citizen and thereby contribute to the great pool of human resource. The faculty members are well qualified, with a sizeable number of them with dual PG degrees and NET/ SLET qualified and pursuing research in the ever-many expanding field of commerce and management. Several faculty members have authored text books and reference books.The Department regularly conducts competitions, workshops and seminars to keep the students abreast of the latest in the world of commerce. Its Business Lab initiative is one of its kind among degree colleges in Bangalore.

The department has a steep cut off percentage in terms of student intake which ensures that there is consistency in quality of teaching and student performance. The department is proud to announce that our college features among the top ten colleges of India for commerce and management and also features in the Top 40 Colleges of India.
