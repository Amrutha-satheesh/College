Bachelor of Arts





The BA in Journalism & mass communication, Psychology and English introduces its students to world-class journalism and media. This programme instils in the students investigative, analytical, and reporting skills, along with a thorough understanding of cultural aspects that shape a society. At Seshadripuram college, this course consolidates a spectrum of theoretical and practical approaches to aid its students in mastering important skills like critical thinking, expression, and research. The curriculum aims to evoke inquisitiveness in its students and exposes them to a broader perspective of society.

The balanced curriculum challenges students to confront various contemporary issues in different fields, develop their analytical skills, and gain hands-on industry experience. This course structure encourages students to explore various fields of journalism like Photojournalism, History of Print Media and Electronic Media, Gender Studies, and Public Relations and Corporate Communications. The holistic learning environment provided at Seshadripuram College ensures that students can become equipped with the knowledge and proficiency that is required for a fruitful professional career.
