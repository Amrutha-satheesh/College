Department of Computer Science





The BCA Department aims to provide key computer skills and abreast the students towards real world technological progress in the domain of computer science .it provides the environment to boost up the employability prospects among our students.

BCA department aims to keeps students well-versed with the rapid global IT developments to provide practical exposure to real world scenarios.

This increasing growth of IT industry has created a lot of opportunities for the computer graduates. It is one of the popular course for the students who want to make it big in the Information Technology field.

The program exposes students to developing, programming, networking, computer and mobile software, app development, etc. It offers great opportunities to students to learn new skills and take advantage of opportunities coming their way.
